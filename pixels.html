What is Pixels?

Pixels is a Web3 farming game where players can explore, farm, craft, and trade in a fun metaverse.

How to Play

  • 🔹 **Create an Account** – Sign up on the official Pixels site.
  • 🌾 **Start Farming** – Plant crops, water them, and harvest.
  • 🏠 **Build & Craft** – Expand your land and craft useful tools.
  • 📈 **Trade & Earn** – Sell your produce and trade with others.
